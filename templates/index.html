To generate larger rep-digit files use the following numbers generator python program.

python3 generate_numbers.py input_file.txt output_file.txt number_of_times
# Example of generating million digit file with rep-digit 1 from 1000 digit file:
python3 generate_numbers.py static/numbers/1-1k.txt static/numbers/1-1m.txt 1000
# Another example from 1 million to 100 million
python3 generate_numbers.py static/numbers/1-1m.txt static/numbers/1-100m.txt 100

WARNING: If you are increasing numbers in billions or 100s of millions the results minght not show up in UI and should be checked at the terminal window.
